About Clozetta

Clozetta is an online fast fashion retailer based in the Philippines. It offers a wide range of trendy and affordable clothing and accessories, catering to fashion-conscious individuals who want to stay up-to-date with the latest styles without breaking the bank.

What?

Clozetta is an online fast fashion retailer based in the Philippines.

Where?

Clozetta's headquarters are situated in Punta Sta. Ana, Manila, Philippines. This location serves as the hub for its operations, including product management, customer service, and logistics.
